Johnston Forum

Johnston Forum is a forum I built for my school in 2016. It uses the PHPBB bulletin board system as well as multiple plug and play extensions. In addition, the UI design and multiple functional upgrades were implemented. The site ran for 5 weeks and garnered 55 active users, 82 public posts (over 500 posts total) and 21 distinct topics.

Johnston Games

Johnston Games aimed to solve a simple problem. My high school gave every student an iPad, and while leisure time was spent playing games on the desktop computers in my school. Because of the iPad’s inability to play flash games, I created a website that hosted webGL and html5 canvas games. For the 5 weeks the site was up, it received 1800 unique visitors and over 25 thousand page views.
